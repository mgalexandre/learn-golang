The schedule

A 6-week study plan

Learn a concept, then apply it immediately — that alternation is the retrieval loop the whole method runs on. Adjust the pace to your life; the order is what matters. Each concept module is ~40 min of reading plus its drills.

WEEK 1 · foundations

Language + first build

Get the basics down and ship something runnable on day one, then sharpen with idiomatic patterns.

L01L08build P01
WEEK 2 · concurrency

Go's headline skill

The most-tested topic. Read L02, then bound it under real constraints in the fetcher.

L02build P02
WEEK 3 · services

APIs + security

net/http, REST, gRPC, GraphQL, and auth. Start the microservice with protected endpoints.

L03L07start P03
WEEK 4 · data

State & messaging

Postgres, Redis, and queues. Wire the persistence and cache layers into the microservice.

L05finish P03
WEEK 5 · production

Ship & observe

Containerize, deploy, and instrument. Testing and observability turn code into a service.

L04L06build P04
WEEK 6 · interview-ready

Scale & polish

System design ties it together. Write the design doc, clean the README, run mock interviews.
